I love Yogurt

Especially Greek Yogurt


What makes Greek yogurt different from the regular kind? They're both rich in probiotics, calcium, vitamins and minerals, but Greek yogurt is strained to remove most of the whey. The result is a thicker, creamier yogurt with twice the protein content, filling you up and keeping you full for longer.You may know that probiotics can improve your gut health, but did you know those benefits extend to your mental health as well? Studies have shown that adding probiotics to your diet (like those found in Greek yogurt) can reduce stress and anxiety.
